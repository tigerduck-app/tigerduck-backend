Export

Bundles pg_dump --format=custom of the tigerduck database plus a manifest into a tigerduck-export-<timestamp>.tar.gz. No secrets are included (APNs .p8, FCM JSON, .env stay on the source machine).

Import

Accepts either a .tar.gz produced by Export above, or a bare pg_dump file from a pre-portal install. The portal does NOT restart the backend for you — after import you'll see a banner here telling you to do it manually.
